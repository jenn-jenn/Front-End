The Red Fox

They use the whiskers on their legs and face to navigate.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average Body Length: 18-35 in
  • Average Lifespan: 2-4 years
  • Habitat: Northern Hemisphere

The red fox is said to be the most populated of the Carnivora family because of its ability to adapt to the environment. Hence, its habitat is quite diverse.
